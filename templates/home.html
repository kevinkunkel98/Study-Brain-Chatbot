About

This is an AI Chatbot Web Application that has been made in the Context of Software Engineering for AI (SE4AI) at the University of Leipzig. Group Members are Yannik Dannies, Fabian Stoehr, Ferdinand Lange and Kevin Kunkel. We are four CS-Students who enjoy building Applications and tinkering around with new Frameworks and Technologies. Study Brain is a Personal Tutor who provide answers to questions for selected lectures.

Features

PDF Support
Upload your own lecture slides to the chatbot to ask questions specifically about its content. We will scrape data that fits to the lecture slides topics.
Chat Performance
Enjoy fast and reliable response times. Don't wait with starting your first conversation!
Big Data
STUDY BRAIN supports a huge data lake from either being feed with the GPT API or dynamically webscraping medium articles.

Getting Started

Step 1: Upload your Lectures Slides on to Study BrAIn.

Step 2: Write a Question Prompt that relates to your Lecture Slides and wait for the Answer.

Step 3: Continue and Develope your Conversation with Study BrAIn.

Step 4: (Optional): Send us your Feedback on your Conversation!
